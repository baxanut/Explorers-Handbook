⚑ Field Intelligence

This week is typical Putrajaya weather — hot and humid with afternoon showers likely most days. Tuesday is the worst day at 85% rain chance, so avoid long expeditions then. Best windows are early mornings before 11am when it's cooler and rain hasn't built up yet.

🌡 Heat Advisory

Highs of 31–32°C all week. Always bring enough water — at least 1.5L per explorer. Take shade breaks.

☔ Rain Readiness

Pack an umbrella or poncho every time. Malaysian rain can come fast — paths get muddy and slippery quickly.

⏰ Best Time to Go

7am–10am is the golden window. Cool enough to move, dry enough to explore. Avoid 2pm–5pm — peak storm time.

👟 Terrain Tip

After rain, grass and soil around Putrajaya wetlands gets very slick. Wear shoes with grip, not sandals.
