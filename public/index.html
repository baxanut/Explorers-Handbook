⚑ Field Intelligence

Typical Putrajaya week — hot and humid with afternoon showers. Tuesday is the worst day at 85% rain. Best window is early morning before 11am when it's cooler and the rain hasn't built up.

🌡 Heat

Highs of 31–32°C. Bring at least 1.5L water per explorer. Take shade breaks.

☔ Rain

Pack a poncho every time. Malaysian rain arrives fast — paths get slippery quick.

⏰ Best Time

7am–10am is the golden window. Avoid 2pm–5pm — peak storm time in Putrajaya.

👟 Terrain

After rain the grass near Putrajaya wetlands gets very slick. Wear grip shoes, not sandals.
